Welcome to the Huating Wang Lab

We are a research group in the Department of Orthopaedics and Traumatology at the CUHK. Our goal is to study gene regulatory mechanisms using skeletal muscle cells and cancer cells as model systems. Currently the main focus of her group is to study the functional roles of non-coding RNAs in regulating gene expression in skeletal muscle stem cells and muscle regeneration.
