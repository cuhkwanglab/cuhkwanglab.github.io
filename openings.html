Open positions

We are currently looking for new group members! We are interested in studying gene regulatory mechanisms in skeletal muscle stem cell and muscle regeneration.

Basic Requirements:

Applicants should have (i) a Bachelor or Master degree in Biomedical Science, Bioinformatics or related discipline; (ii) A self-motivated and diligent person. (iii) Experience in genome-wide study, RNA work, and mouse genetics is desired; (iv) Admission requirement: proof of proficiency in English: TOEFL exam (79 or above for the Internet-based test), or IELTS >6.5; good GPA; valid degree certificates. If interested, please contact Dr. Huating Wang. Short listed candidates will be interviewed in person or by phone call. A monthly stipend of ~HKD18,000 will be offered to support the entire study period starting 2024.
